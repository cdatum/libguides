Here are the steps you can take to integrate YouTube videos into your PowerPoint presentations. Depending on which version of PowerPoint you're using, click the appropriate option below:

PowerPoint 2016

  1. Find a video in YouTube and get its embed code:

  2. In PowerPoint, click the Insert tab > Video > Online Video
  3. In the “From a Video Embed Code” section, paste in the embed code copied from YouTube:

PowerPoint 2013

  1. Find a video in YouTube and get its embed code:

  2. In PowerPoint, Click the Insert tab > Video > Online Video
  3. In the “From a Video Embed Code” section, paste in the embed code copied from YouTube:

PowerPoint 2010

  1. Make the Developer tab visible:
    Click File > Options > Customize Ribbon > in the column on the right, place a check next to Developer > click OK.
  2. On Developer tab select the “More controls” button

     
  3. Choose “Shockwave Flash Object" and click OK
  4. Draw a box on the slide (this is where your video will appear)
  5. Right click the box and choose “Properties”
  6. In the “Movie” field, paste the Youtube URL
  7. Modify the highlighted portions of the URL you just pasted from this:

    Make sure the URL begins with http instead of https
    http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=57qB3fjt48I

    To this:

    http://www.youtube.com/v/57qB3fjt48I
     

  8. Optional: To start the above video at the 1m 10sec mark, add &start=70 to the end of the URL like this:
    http://www.youtube.com/v/57qB3fjt48I&start=70

    (1min 10sec:  1 minute = 60 seconds, 60 + 10 seconds = 70 seconds)
     
  9. Close the properties box and preview your slide in presentation mode (Shift + F5)
