Overview

The steps outlined below will allow faculty members to embed a wide variety of content from the Library’s LibGuides system into their Blackboard course. Types of content that can be embedded include complete guides, single pages, individual content boxes, a research database, or a list of research databases for a specific subject area. Knowing which content pieces you want to embed is an important prerequisite.

1. In a content area, click “Build Content“and then choose the “CCAC Library Resources Content Tool” option.
 
2. Provide a name and description for the link that you will be adding.

In this example, we will be adding the LibGuide that has the APA Style guide resources. Having an accurate name and description will let students know the purpose of the link.

Click “Submit” when ready.


 
3. After the link is created, click it to open the content selection panel. In this example, the link that was created in step 2 is the “APA Citation Style” link.


 
4. On the content selection panel, use the drop down menus to locate the page or resource that you want to provide. If you need assistance finding a resource, get in touch with the library.

After selecting the content, click the “Embed Content” button to submit.


 
5. Once the content is successfully embedded, navigate back to the content item and click the link again to open the View & Edit window.


 
6. Note that students will not see this View & Edit screen. In this example, when a student clicks the “APA Citation Style” link, they will be taken directly to the content.

Instructors, however, will need to click the View Content link to see the content.

Instructors can also click the “Edit Selection” to select different content from the LibGuides system.

Get in touch with the Library if you encounter any issues or have questions.
